Storyline

A cat has just escaped from a place where it is not happy. The cat is on a journey to find a place that will bring it happiness.

Chapter 1: New journey?
There are 21 maps. Each map shows major cities in Vietnam.
In chapter one, the cat will travel to the big cities of Vietnam. With the hope that in this place, he can find a chance to change his life.

Chapter 2: Can find joy?
There are 21 maps. Each map is a beautiful city of Vietnam.
Chapter two is the continuation of the cat's journey. He encountered beautiful scenes, he immersed himself in the nature of those places. Those natural pictures probably made him open his heart more and feel the smallest joys.

Chapter 3: What is happiness?
There are 21 maps. Each map is a place the cat has never been to.
Chapter three will be the final stages of the journey. Here the cat will begin to think about the past, present and future: “Does happiness really exist? If it exists, how can we reach it?”.

The end of the whole journey will be the answer to the cat's thoughts. Looking back at the whole process, the places he has been, the beautiful sceneries, the stories behind, and the pitiful situations like his.
